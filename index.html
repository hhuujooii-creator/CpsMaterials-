Welcome to CPS Materials

Located in Missouri and proudly operating since 1995, CPS Materials specializes in custom plastic manufacturing for businesses requiring large-scale production. We transform your technical drawings and dimensions into high-quality plastic components built to meet your exact specifications.

Whether you need bottle caps, industrial components, or custom molded plastic parts, our experienced team ensures precision, reliability, and consistent production output.

About Us

CPS Materials was founded in 1995 and is managed by Owen Carroway. Our facility operates with a team of 9 skilled employees dedicated to delivering reliable plastic manufacturing solutions.

We proudly serve Missouri and neighboring states with dependable production contracts lasting weeks or months depending on client needs.

Our Manufacturing Process

We specialize in plastic injection molding and custom tooling production. Our process includes mold preparation, plastic pellet melting, injection, cooling, and strict quality inspection.

Custom Plastic Products

We manufacture large production runs of custom-designed plastic parts based on client-provided plans and measurements.

  • Bottle Caps
  • Industrial Plastic Components
  • Custom Molded Parts
  • Contract Manufacturing Production

Certifications & Quality

CPS Materials operates under ISO-certified standards, ensuring consistent production quality, safety compliance, and reliable performance for all manufacturing contracts.
